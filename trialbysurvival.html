Description

You have been banished to the wasteland to prove your innocence. Your Trial By Survival has begun. How will you survive?
Trial By Survival is a post-apocalyptic, top-down action RPG set in an overgrown world long after civilization has collapsed. Navigate through lush destructible environments to find what you need to survive. Scavenge ammunition and supplies from other characters. Build and level weapons to confront the inevitable danger that lurks after every turn. Use the character enhancing perks to fine tune your playing style and evolve your tactics as the game develops around you to escape permanent death. Every game is a unique story that writes itself and will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Features

  • Multiple Game Modes - Survival Mode and Arena Mode
  • Permadeath
  • Procedurally generated map - every game is unique
  • Destructible environments - Break down walls, blow up cars, set the world on fire
  • Move freely around randomized levels, fighting through indoor and outdoor encounters
  • Scavenge loot from real life players around the world
  • Glitch the dog will help defend you, fetch things for you and keep you company
  • Build and level up over a dozen unique weapons
  • Customize your playing style with a character enhancing Perk system
  • Great Comic/graphic art style
  • Dual Stick or Drag/Tap Controls
  • No network connection needed to play

Additional Info

  • The game uses a scavenging system in place of an in-game store. When a character dies, their inventory is dropped at that map point for other players to scavenge. If a player is running low on ammo they can take a chance at another player’s inventory.
  • In the dynamic environment cars can blow up and set zombies and houses on fire. It can spread from zombie to zombie. Walls can be knocked down to create an impromptu escape route, or inadvertently give zombies another angle of attack. Players will feel satisfied leaving the entire area in rubble after a hard battle.
  • Trial By Survival has a combat system that is great for mobile platforms. The character will automatically defend themselves against one or two enemies. Glitch, the dog will help defend as well. It’s a system where if the player miss taps or get distracted for a second, they aren’t doomed to lose all their progress. One enemy isn’t a threat. How 10 enemies are handled is what will determine whether you succeed or fail.
  • The player will feel like a bad ass playing the game. The guns kick, bullets will knock back enemies and break windows. You barrel through windows and kick down doors. The world is very kinetic and responsive to the player’s actions.
  • Trial By Survival has two game modes, Survival Mode and Arena Mode that complement each other. The scavenging system in Survival Mode requires coins that can be earned in Arena Mode, and weapons can be unlocked for Arena Mode through progress in Survival Mode.
  • The graphic art style of Trial By Survival is influenced by comics. The world is lush with overgrowth as nature takes over where civilization once was. Vines dangle from power lines and flowers grow out into the street. This environment is enhanced by subtle environmental effects and punctuated by realistic sounds and special effects for combat.
